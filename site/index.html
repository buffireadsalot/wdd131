Our Legacy

The Buffin family's heritage is marked by tragedy, resilience, and, in the wake of it all, an incredible legacy of strength and beauty. Buffin Ancestry is not just a place to highlight our many branches; it's a place where we can come together, share memories, find information, and revel in the rich past that makes us who we are today.

Not only do I want to catalog and showcase my family heritage, but it would be a pleasure to help you showcase yours. Let's work together to grow the branches of your family tree.
